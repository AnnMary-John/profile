About Me

My journey started as a developer in 2021, and since then, I've dived deep into building scalable web and mobile applications. Whether it's crafting sleek UI, engineering efficient backends, or deploying polished products, I love the entire build cycle. Along the way, I’ve explored technologies like Flutter, React Native, and more — always staying curious and hungry to learn.

When I’m not coding, I’m either catching a movie, going on a night ride, or enjoying ice cream while planning my next side project. You can find me on GitHub, where I share what I build, or catch glimpses of my journey on Dev.to.
